# Daily-Animal-Facts-with-Pixel

Daily Animal fact #1

Did you know skunks are nearsighted animals? Skunks can only see objects up to 10 feet away!

Because of their poor poor eyesight skunks rely on their sense of smell to help them navigate the world.

Did you also know that there are 12 living species of skunks in the world? Skunks are pretty cool!
